Hi there! I am Gladys Maniago. I live in Las Vegas and currently learning Front End Development at The Iron Yard in the ever blooming downtown area. I never thought that I would be going to school to become a developer and here I am now enjoying every minute of it. There is something very satisfying about staring at a blank page and making something off of it.

This website will be showcasing all the things I have learned thus far, what my interests are and some tidbits about me. With the use of HTML, CSS and Javascript, I hope to bring life to a website using all the techniques, skills and best practices I have discovered. My goals are to master these three programs by the end of the course and to keep learning. Stopping there would just be boring!

Please join me in my journey as I learn to develop learn, breathe and dream code one pixel at a time.
