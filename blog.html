Final Fantasy VII Remake Announced!

So I know I am a bit late in realizing this news, but I cannot fully express just how excited I am. The official announcement was made on June 15. The original game was the first ever game I played in the series. I just remember a younger me buying my first second hand PlayStation when I was 14. Needless to say I am taken back to those summer days where I shamelessly spent majority of my time playing the game. I was obsesssed.

I am sharing the same feeling as us countless fans await its release. I am denifinetly stoked. As excited I was when the first Lord of the Rings film rolled out. FF7 is probably one of my favorite RPG games and it's probably fortunate that the remake will not be coming into store shelves until 2016 because if it did come out this year. I am not sure if I'll have enough discipline to study for my Front End class and work at the same time. It should be expected that when the time comes, my old habits of holing in my room day in day out will come back.

Check out the trailer and join me in the geek out!
